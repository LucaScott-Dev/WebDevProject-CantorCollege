Explore Cantor College

At Cantor College, we are committed to providing our students with the best possible environment to learn, create, and innovate. Our state-of-the-art facilities are designed to support your academic journey and help you thrive in your chosen field. Whether you’re studying computing, design, or technology, our campus offers everything you need to excel.

Our Facilities

1. Advanced Computing Labs

Our computing labs are equipped with the latest hardware and software, giving you the tools to develop your skills in programming, cybersecurity, data science, and more. The labs are accessible 24/7, so you can work whenever inspiration strikes.

2. Design Studios

Our design studios offer a creative space for students in graphic design, digital media, and product design. Each studio includes high-end graphic tablets, professional software, and large-format printers. Collaborative spaces also allow you to work on group projects with peers and lecturers.

3. Innovation and Makerspace

Cantor College’s Innovation and Makerspace is a hub for creativity and invention. With 3D printers, laser cutters, CNC machines, and advanced prototyping tools, this space allows you to turn ideas into reality—perfect for both coursework and personal projects.

4. Technology Sandbox

The Technology Sandbox is an experimental space dedicated to VR, AR, and AI development. With access to cutting-edge devices and software, you can explore emerging technologies and push the boundaries of what's possible in your field.

5. Collaborative Learning Spaces

Our campus features numerous collaborative learning spaces designed for teamwork and problem-solving. These areas include smartboards, video conferencing tools, and flexible seating arrangements—ideal for group work and study sessions.

6. Library and Resource Centre

The Cantor College Library offers an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources relating to computing, design, and technology. With quiet study areas, computer stations, and access to online databases, it’s the perfect place to research, revise, and develop ideas.

7. Student Hub

The Student Hub is the social centre of our campus. It features a café, lounge areas, and recreational facilities—making it a great place to relax between classes, meet with friends, or get involved in clubs and student-led activities.

8. Career and Development Centre

Our Career and Development Centre is dedicated to preparing you for your future career. With career counselling, CV workshops, job-placement support, and regular employer networking events, the Centre provides everything you need to make the transition from student to professional.

9. Fitness and Wellness Centre

We promote both mental and physical wellbeing. The Fitness and Wellness Centre includes a fully equipped gym, studio spaces for exercise classes, and access to wellness services such as yoga, meditation, and counselling.

10. On-Campus Housing

Cantor College offers modern, comfortable on-campus accommodation. Residence halls provide high-speed internet, study lounges, and communal areas, creating a supportive and friendly residential community.

Experience Cantor College

Our facilities are designed to enhance your learning experience and support your academic and personal growth. We invite you to explore our campus, discover the resources available to you, and make the most of everything Cantor College has to offer.
